Computer Vision and Machine Learning based Mine Detection and Mapping Robot using Aruco Markers

In our research, we developed a robotic system that employs computer vision and machine learning for effective mine detection and mapping, utilizing Aruco markers for enhanced navigation and localization. This approach significantly reduces human risk and increases the effectiveness of demining efforts by providing precise, reliable data.

Advanced AI Image Processing and Payload Delivery with an Autonomous UAV

In our research, we developed a fully autonomous Unmanned Aerial Vehicle (UAV) leverages machine learning to process and analyze aerial imagery, and disaster response. Additionally, it features a sophisticated mechanism for accurate payload delivery, enabling efficient supply drops and support to remote or hard-to-reach locations.

Dielectric barrier discharge DC-pulsating plasma flow control: experimental and numerical studies

In our study, We focus on how DBD plasma, modulated by direct current pulsations, can influence aerodynamic flows, particularly in terms of boundary layer modification and drag reduction. Our experimental findings are rigorously analyzed and juxtaposed with simulations to assess the efficacy and reliability of this plasma-based flow control strategy.
